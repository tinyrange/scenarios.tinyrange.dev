TinyRange Scenarios (Beta)

About

TinyRange is a lightweight Cyber Range designed to run ready-to-go cyber security scenarios on a wide range of computer systems. These scenarios come in the form of Starlark scripts.

TinyRange has been developed at The University of Queensland within UQ Cyber by Joshua D. Scarsbrook. The runtime is not currently publicly available but we are intending to release it shortly.

This website hosts a variety of scenarios covering different skills and TinyRange features.
